Thank you for downloading EPStack!

EPStack is a ready-to-deploy log management solution. This is a scalable solution that offers much more than just log management. It is capable of a variety of inputs and can be used for a wide spectrum of use cases. We have taken the hassle of setup and configuration of ELK stack away so you can concentrate on what’s most important – collecting the data and start analyzing.

Highlights of EPStack

  • Admin Panel with GUI based configuration editor
  • User Management
  • Supports various inputs and formats
  • Manage unstructured data
